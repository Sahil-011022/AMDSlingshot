# Requirements Document: MediCheck

## Introduction

MediCheck is an AI-powered healthcare platform that connects patients, doctors, and healthcare facilities. The system enables patients to analyze medical symptoms through multiple input modalities (text, voice, images), generates comprehensive medical reports, and facilitates secure communication between patients and healthcare providers. The platform provides role-based access for three distinct user types: Patients, Doctors, and Clinics/Hospitals.

## Glossary

- **MediCheck_Platform**: The complete healthcare application system
- **Patient**: A user who seeks medical symptom analysis and healthcare services
- **Doctor**: A healthcare provider who reviews patient reports and provides medical guidance
- **Clinic**: A healthcare facility that manages appointments and patient care
- **Symptom_Analyzer**: The AI component that processes patient symptoms and generates reports
- **Medical_Report**: A comprehensive document generated by the AI based on symptom analysis
- **Emergency_Contact**: Critical healthcare service contact information (ambulance, emergency response, etc.)
- **AI_Buddy**: Conversational AI assistant for patient support
- **Report_History**: Collection of all medical reports generated for a patient
- **Doctor_Inbox**: Communication channel between patients and doctors
- **Authentication_System**: Role-based user authentication and authorization component
- **Input_Modality**: Method of symptom input (text, voice, or image)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: User Authentication and Role Management

**User Story:** As a user, I want to sign in with my specific role (Patient, Doctor, or Clinic), so that I can access role-appropriate features and data.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user attempts to sign in, THE Authentication_System SHALL verify their credentials and assigned role
2. WHEN authentication succeeds, THE Authentication_System SHALL grant access only to features authorized for that role
3. WHEN a user session is established, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL maintain role-based permissions throughout the session
4. IF authentication fails, THEN THE Authentication_System SHALL reject access and provide an error message
5. THE Authentication_System SHALL encrypt and securely store user credentials

### Requirement 2: Multi-Modal Symptom Input

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to describe my symptoms through text, voice, or images, so that I can communicate my health concerns in the most convenient way.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient selects text input, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide a text interface for symptom description
2. WHEN a Patient selects voice input, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL capture and transcribe audio to text
3. WHEN a Patient uploads an image, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL accept common image formats (JPEG, PNG, HEIC)
4. WHEN voice input is processed, THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL convert speech to text with accuracy sufficient for medical analysis
5. WHEN an image is uploaded, THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL extract visual symptom information from the image
6. IF an uploaded file exceeds size limits or is an unsupported format, THEN THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL reject the upload and notify the Patient

### Requirement 3: AI-Powered Symptom Analysis

**User Story:** As a patient, I want the AI to analyze my symptoms and generate a comprehensive medical report, so that I can understand my condition and share it with healthcare providers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN symptom input is received, THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL process the input and generate a Medical_Report
2. THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL include symptom summary, potential conditions, severity assessment, and recommendations in the Medical_Report
3. WHEN analysis is complete, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL store the Medical_Report in the Patient's Report_History
4. THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL complete analysis within a reasonable timeframe (under 30 seconds for standard cases)
5. IF symptom input is insufficient or unclear, THEN THE Symptom_Analyzer SHALL request additional information from the Patient

### Requirement 4: Report Sharing with Doctors

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to send my medical reports to doctors on the platform, so that I can receive professional medical guidance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient selects a Medical_Report to share, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display a list of available Doctors
2. WHEN a Patient sends a report to a Doctor, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL deliver the report to the Doctor's inbox
3. WHEN a report is sent, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL notify the Doctor of the new report
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL maintain a record of which reports were sent to which Doctors
5. WHEN a Doctor receives a report, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display the complete Medical_Report with all symptom details

### Requirement 5: Doctor Report Review

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to receive and review patient reports in my inbox, so that I can provide medical guidance and respond to patient concerns.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient sends a report, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL add it to the Doctor's Doctor_Inbox
2. WHEN a Doctor accesses their inbox, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display all received reports with patient information
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Doctors to view full Medical_Report details
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL enable Doctors to respond to patients through the Doctor_Inbox
5. WHEN a Doctor views a report, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL mark it as read

### Requirement 6: Emergency Care Access

**User Story:** As a patient, I want quick access to emergency contacts and crisis help, so that I can get immediate assistance in critical situations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display Emergency_Contact information prominently on the patient dashboard
2. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide contacts for Ambulance (102), Emergency Response (108), Mental Health support, and Poison Control
3. WHEN a Patient accesses emergency care, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display contact information immediately without requiring navigation
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL enable direct calling or messaging to emergency services where supported
5. THE Emergency_Contact information SHALL be accessible even with limited network connectivity

### Requirement 7: Patient Dashboard Features

**User Story:** As a patient, I want a comprehensive dashboard with all healthcare tools, so that I can manage my health needs from one place.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide a patient dashboard with access to symptom analysis, doctor finder, AI_Buddy, pharmacy finder, appointments, and reports
2. WHEN a Patient logs in, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display the dashboard as the primary interface
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL organize dashboard features in a clear, accessible layout
4. WHEN a Patient selects a dashboard feature, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL navigate to that feature within 2 seconds
5. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display the Patient's recent activity and pending actions on the dashboard

### Requirement 8: Report History Management

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to access all my previous medical reports, so that I can track my health history and share past reports with doctors.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL maintain a Report_History for each Patient containing all generated Medical_Reports
2. WHEN a Patient accesses Report_History, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display reports in reverse chronological order
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Patients to view, download, and share reports from their history
4. WHEN a Patient searches Report_History, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL filter reports by date, symptoms, or conditions
5. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL preserve Report_History indefinitely unless explicitly deleted by the Patient

### Requirement 9: AI Buddy Conversational Assistant

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to interact with an AI assistant, so that I can get health information and guidance through natural conversation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient accesses AI_Buddy, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide a conversational interface
2. THE AI_Buddy SHALL respond to patient questions about health, symptoms, and platform features
3. THE AI_Buddy SHALL maintain conversation context throughout a session
4. WHEN AI_Buddy provides medical information, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL include appropriate disclaimers about seeking professional medical advice
5. THE AI_Buddy SHALL respond to patient messages within 5 seconds

### Requirement 10: Doctor and Pharmacy Finder

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to find doctors and pharmacies near me, so that I can access healthcare services conveniently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient uses the doctor finder, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display available Doctors with their specialties and availability
2. WHEN a Patient uses the pharmacy finder, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display nearby pharmacies with contact information
3. WHERE location services are enabled, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL prioritize results based on proximity to the Patient
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Patients to filter doctors by specialty, availability, and ratings
5. WHEN a Patient selects a doctor or pharmacy, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display detailed information and contact options

### Requirement 11: Appointment Booking

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to book appointments with doctors or clinics, so that I can schedule healthcare visits.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Patient requests an appointment, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL display available time slots from the selected Doctor or Clinic
2. WHEN a Patient confirms an appointment, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL reserve the time slot and notify the Doctor or Clinic
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL send appointment confirmation to both Patient and healthcare provider
4. WHEN an appointment is booked, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL add it to both the Patient's and provider's calendars
5. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Patients to cancel or reschedule appointments with appropriate notice

### Requirement 12: Secure Healthcare Data Handling

**User Story:** As a user, I want my medical data to be securely stored and transmitted, so that my privacy and confidentiality are protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL encrypt all Medical_Reports and patient data at rest
2. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL encrypt all data transmissions using industry-standard protocols (TLS 1.3 or higher)
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL restrict access to patient data based on role and explicit permissions
4. WHEN data is shared between users, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL maintain an audit log of access
5. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL comply with healthcare data protection regulations (HIPAA, GDPR as applicable)

### Requirement 13: User Profile Management

**User Story:** As a user, I want to manage my profile information, so that I can keep my account details current and accurate.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow users to view and edit their profile information
2. WHEN a user updates profile information, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL validate the changes before saving
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Patients to add medical history, allergies, and current medications to their profile
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Doctors to add credentials, specialties, and availability to their profile
5. WHEN profile changes are saved, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL update the information across all relevant platform features

### Requirement 14: Doctor-Patient Communication

**User Story:** As a patient or doctor, I want to communicate securely through the platform, so that I can discuss medical concerns and receive guidance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide a messaging interface within the Doctor_Inbox for two-way communication
2. WHEN a message is sent, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL deliver it to the recipient and send a notification
3. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL maintain message history for each patient-doctor conversation
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL encrypt all messages in transit and at rest
5. WHEN a user sends a message, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL confirm delivery with a timestamp

### Requirement 15: Clinic/Hospital Management

**User Story:** As a clinic administrator, I want to manage facility information and appointments, so that I can coordinate patient care effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Clinics to manage their facility information, services, and operating hours
2. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Clinics to view and manage appointment schedules
3. WHEN a Clinic updates availability, THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL reflect changes in the appointment booking system immediately
4. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL allow Clinics to assign doctors to appointments
5. THE MediCheck_Platform SHALL provide Clinics with patient appointment history and reports when authorized
